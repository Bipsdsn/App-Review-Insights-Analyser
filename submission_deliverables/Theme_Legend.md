# Theme Legend

- **General Praise & Sentiment** — Reviews that express overall satisfaction or dissatisfaction without a specific topic
- **Payment Failures** — Reviews that mention issues with failed or problematic transactions
- **KYC / Verification** — Reviews that discuss issues related to Know Your Customer verification or account setup
- **Fees and Charges** — Reviews that mention concerns about extra charges, fees, or pricing for services like mobile recharges
- **App Performance and Support** — Reviews that discuss issues with the app's speed, functionality, or the quality of customer support
